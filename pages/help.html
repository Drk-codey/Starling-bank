FAQ
How do I create an account?

To create an account, visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and password. Follow the prompts to complete the registration process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and digital wallets such as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Simply choose your preferred payment method at checkout.

How can I send money to another user?

Sending money to another user is easy. Log in to your account, navigate to the "Send Money" section, and enter the recipient's email address or phone number. Specify the amount you want to send and confirm the transaction. The recipient will receive a notification and can claim the funds.

What are the fees for using your service?

We strive to offer competitive and transparent pricing. Our fee structure varies depending on the type of transaction and the payment method used. You can find detailed information on our pricing page or by contacting our support team.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions on how to reset your password. Follow the provided link and set a new password for your account.

How can I view my transaction history?

To view your transaction history, log in to your account and navigate to the "Transaction History" or "Activity" section. Here, you can access a detailed record of your past transactions, including dates, amounts, and transaction IDs.
